Why others see "App not active"

Your Meta app is in Development mode. Only admins, developers, and testers can sign in.

Add each user as a tester (temporary) or publish the app (permanent, for anyone).

Option A — Add as tester

  1. developers.facebook.com/apps → your app
  2. App roles → Roles → Add testers
  3. They accept the invite in Facebook Settings → Business integrations

Option B — Public access

  1. Deploy to HTTPS with a Privacy Policy URL
  2. App Review — request pages_show_list, pages_messaging, etc.
  3. Complete Business Verification if required
  4. Add production redirect URI to Facebook Login for Business
  5. Toggle app In development → Live

Option C — Message old inquirers (Reply'em style)

To broadcast outside the 24-hour window, Meta requires Human Agent advanced access.

  1. developers.facebook.com/apps → your app → App Review → Permissions and Features
  2. Request advanced access for Human Agent and pages_messaging
  3. Upload a screen recording showing your app sending a tagged reply to a past inquiry
  4. After approval, use All past inquirers (Message Tag) mode in this app

App Review usually takes days to weeks. Until then, use testers for anyone who needs access now.
